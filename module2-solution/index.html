Kitfo

Kitfo is a traditional food of Ethiopia. it is a speciality of a cultural group called the 'Guragae'. It contains minced beef, spices(coriandor,chillie) and butter. could be presented medium rare or rare.

Chechebsa

Chechebsa is a traditional food of Ethiopia. It got it origin from a tribe in Ethiopia refered to as the 'Oromo'. It contains a thin pita bread made into pieces and mixed well with spices and butter.

Doro-Wot

Doro-Wot is a very popular food in Ethiopia. It is chicken made in a traditional way in the form of stew. It is usually eaten during a certain holiday. it contains Chicken, Oninos, Chille, Peper, Garlic, Butter and Oil.
